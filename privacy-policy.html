PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: March 05, 2026

Zaralla Studios ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you play TwinHit: Split-Brain Arcade.

1. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect or store any personal data directly on our own servers. Our game is built using Unity 6, and we use Google Play Games Services to provide core features such as leaderboards and achievements.

2. Third-Party Services

To enhance your experience, our app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services: Used for game authentication, cloud saves, and global leaderboards.
  • Unity: Used as the game engine, which may collect technical device information for performance optimization.

You can review their privacy policies here:

Google Privacy Policy Unity Privacy Policy

3. Data Deletion (User Rights)

In compliance with Google Play Store policies, we provide a clear path for users to manage and delete their data. Since your game data (scores and profile) is managed through Google Play Games Services, you can delete it at any time by following these steps:

  1. Open the Google Play Games app on your Android device.
  2. Tap on Settings.
  3. Select "Delete Play Games account & data".
  4. Find TwinHit under "Delete individual game data" and tap Delete.

Once this process is completed, your scores will be permanently removed from our global leaderboards.

4. Children's Privacy

Our games are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and discover that your child has provided data to Google Play Services through our game, please contact the service provider or us.
